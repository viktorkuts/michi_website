## What is Michi?
Michi shows you what's happening near you: real plans, posted by real people, close enough to actually go. Think pickup basketball or a day at the lake. There's no feed to scroll and no follower counts, just what's on around you.

## How do I sign up?
Download Michi from the App Store or Play Store and sign in with your Google or Apple account. Pick a few interests, tell us when in the week you're usually free, and you're in.

## What does Michi cost?
Nothing. Michi is free, has no ads, and doesn't sell your data.

## Where does Michi work?
We're rolling out city by city. Open the app to see if your area is live, or join the waitlist for it. New cities open every month.

## Do I need to be 18?
Yes. Michi is 18+.

## What if my friends aren't on Michi?
You don't need them to be. Michi is built for meeting the people who show up to the same thing you do. Bring a friend along if you want, but you can start alone tonight.
