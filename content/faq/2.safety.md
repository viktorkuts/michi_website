## How does Michi keep things safe?
Every account is tied to a real Google or Apple account, and profiles carry a name and a photo, so hosts and guests know who they're meeting. Hosts approve who joins their events, and nobody sees an event's exact address until they're in.

## How do I report someone?
Open their profile, tap the menu, choose Report. A real person reviews every report, and we respond within 24 hours.

## What happens if I block someone?
They can't see your profile, message you, or join any event you're in. They aren't told they were blocked.

## Can I see who else is going before I show up?
Yes. Every event shows the host, the headcount, and who has RSVP'd before you join.

## Is my exact location ever shared?
No. Before you join, an event only shows the area it's in, and the exact address appears once you've joined. Michi never shows your own location to anyone.
