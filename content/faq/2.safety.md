## How does Synq keep things safe?
Every account is verified by phone number. Hosts can require a verified photo ID for any plan. We screen plan content for prohibited activity before it goes live.

## How do I report someone?
Open their profile, tap the menu, choose Report. Reports go to a real person — we respond within 24 hours.

## What happens if I block someone?
They can't see your profile, message you, or join any plan you're in. They aren't told they were blocked.

## Can I see who else is going before I show up?
Yes. Every plan shows the host, the headcount, and the other RSVPs before you join.

## What if someone no-shows?
Hosts can flag no-shows. Repeated no-shows lower account standing and limit access to popular plans.

## Is my exact location ever shared?
No. Plans show a neighborhood until you RSVP, then the address. We never broadcast where you live.
