## How do I delete my account?
Go to your profile, open Settings, then Account, and tap "Delete account". If you can't reach the app, you can also request deletion by email; the Account Deletion page in the footer walks through it.

## Why are friends capped at 365?
Because real relationships take maintenance, and 365 is already a lot of people to actually see. Friends you haven't interacted with in about a year drop off on their own, so the number stays honest.

## How do I add friends?
There's no search bar for people. You add friends through events you've both been to, through a group chat, or by syncing in person. If you haven't met, you can't add them.

## What do people see on my profile?
Your username, photo, bio, how many events you've joined and hosted, and your third places, the spots you're a regular at. There's no follower count because there are no followers.
