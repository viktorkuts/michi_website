## How do I delete my account?
Settings → Account → Delete account. Your data is removed within 30 days. You can also email contact@synqtogether.com.

## How do I change my phone number?
Settings → Account → Change number. You'll verify the new number by text.

## Why is the friend list capped?
Because Synq is about the people you actually see, not the people you've ever met. The cap is 50.

## Can I export my data?
Yes. Settings → Privacy → Request data export. We send a download link by email within 7 days.

## Will Synq sell or share my data?
No. Synq has no ads and no data partners. Your contact list, location, and activity stay on your account — see the privacy policy for the full breakdown.

## Will Synq notify me when my friends post?
There are no posts. Synq notifies you about plans, not activity feeds.
