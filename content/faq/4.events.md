## How do I create an event?
Tap the plus icon on the Discover page. Pick a place, set a time, write a one-line description, and post it. You can invite friends directly or let people nearby find it.

## How do I join an event?
Tap Join on any event in your Discover page. The host gets your request, and you're in once they accept. If someone shared the event with you directly, there's no approval step; you're in immediately. Once you've joined, you can see the full details and chat with the other attendees.

## What kinds of events work best?
Specific ones. "Tennis at Parc Elm, Thursday 6pm" fills up; "anyone want to play tennis sometime?" doesn't. A clear activity, place, and time makes it easy for people to say yes.

## What happens to the group chat after?
It stays. The chat is the club: you keep talking with the people you met, and when you want to run the same thing again, you link the next event straight to that chat. That's how a Thursday game becomes a weekly thing.
