## How do I create a plan?
Tap the plus icon. Pick a place. Set a time. Write a one-line description. Done.

## How do I join a plan?
Open the plan, tap RSVP. The plan goes in your calendar, the group chat opens, you go.

## Can I cancel an RSVP?
Up to 2 hours before the plan starts. After that, you're marked as no-show if you don't make it.

## What kinds of plans work best?
Specific, doable, public. "Coffee at Maru, Saturday 10am" beats "Anyone want to grab coffee sometime?" The clearer the plan, the more likely strangers actually show up.

## What happens to the group chat after?
It quiets down 24 hours after the plan ends. You can still open it, but no new notifications.

## Can I host a recurring plan?
Yes. When you create a plan, choose "weekly" or "every other week."
