## What we collect

- **Account info.** Phone number, optional name, optional profile photo.
- **Plan activity.** Plans you create, RSVP to, host, or cancel.
- **Device info.** Operating system, app version, crash reports.
- **Location.** Only the city you're in. We never store precise GPS
  coordinates server-side.

## How we use it

- To match you with relevant plans nearby.
- To send notifications about plans you've joined or hosted.
- To screen for safety and abuse.
- To debug crashes and improve the app.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We do not run ads.

## Your rights

- Request a copy of your data: Settings → Privacy → Request export.
- Delete your account: Settings → Account → Delete account.
- Email us: [contact@synqtogether.com](mailto:contact@synqtogether.com).

## Contact

Privacy questions go to [contact@synqtogether.com](mailto:contact@synqtogether.com).
